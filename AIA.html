Table 2: Audience Analysis

Audiences Profile/Scenarios Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Art Collectors Predominantly aged 30-60, high income, experienced in art investment. Prefer using high-resolution displays and fast internet to view art details. High-quality images of artwork, art investment information, authentication and purchasing process. High-resolution image gallery, informative content on art collection, secure online purchasing system.
Art Enthusiasts Young adults to middle-aged, interested in art and culture. Access the internet primarily through mobile devices. Looking for educational content and community engagement. Mobile-friendly website, educational content about art techniques and history, community features like forums or social media integration. Responsive design, blog or video tutorials on art, integration with social media for sharing and discussions.
Aspiring Artists Aged 18-35, students or early-career artists. Seek inspiration and guidance. Limited budget, access internet via laptops or tablets, moderate to high computer skills. Affordable art workshops or courses, inspiration and tips for improving their art, opportunities to showcase their own work. Section for online workshops and tutorials, guest posts or features from established artists, user-generated gallery for community critique and exposure.
Gallery Owners Professionals, aged 35-65, high income. Use desktops with large monitors for business purposes. Interested in discovering new talent and arranging exhibitions. Easy navigation to artist's portfolio and biography, contact information for inquiries and collaborations, exhibition history and future events. Dedicated portfolio section with artist statement and CV, contact form easily accessible, events calendar for upcoming exhibitions and workshops.
