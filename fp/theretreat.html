"The Retreat"

“The retreat” - india ink on paper At a time when I felt at my lowest and least inspired, I took the time to finally express it through art. Despite its lack of color, for the first time ever, I felt as though I was finally able to accurately create a piece that represented how I had been feeling at that time in my life.
